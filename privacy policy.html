Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

At **EK-Alpha Solutions**, your privacy and trust are our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services. By using our site, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • **Name**
  • **Email address**
  • **Phone number**
  • **Physical address**
  • **Information required for insurance purposes** (such as date of birth, identification details, health and vehicle information)

We also collect certain technical data automatically when you use our site, such as IP address, browser type, and cookies, to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To provide, process, and manage your requested services (insurance, dispatch, web development, marketing, etc.)
  • To send service-related updates and notifications
  • To improve our website and customer experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With **trusted third parties**, such as insurance providers and brokers, to deliver services requested by you.
  • With **legal authorities**, if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. International Clients

We serve customers in the U.S. and internationally. By providing your information, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data across borders in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your data.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve functionality
  • Analyze site traffic (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel)
  • Deliver personalized marketing

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Depending on your location (e.g., EU, California), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Restrict or object to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@ekalpha.com.
