General Questions

Q1. What services does EK-Alpha Solutions provide?

We offer **Truck Dispatch Services**, **Insurance Solutions** (Medicare, Home & Auto), **Web Development**, and **Social Media Marketing**. Our goal is to be a one-stop solution for individuals and businesses.

Q2. Do you serve international clients?

Yes. While our primary base is in the United States, we also serve international clients who require web, marketing, and dispatch solutions.

Q3. How can I contact EK-Alpha Solutions?

You can reach us via phone, email, or through the Contact Us page on our website. We also provide **24/7 customer support**.

Q4. What makes you different from others?

Our focus on **affordability**, **speed**, **innovation**, and **customer support** sets us apart. We don’t just deliver services—we build long-term partnerships.

Truck Dispatch FAQs

Q1. What documents are required to start dispatching?

You’ll need your **MC/DOT number**, **insurance certificate**, **W9 form**, and **carrier packet** to get started.

Q2. Do you provide 24/7 dispatch support?

Yes, our dispatch team is available **around the clock** to ensure your trucks stay moving.

Q3. How do you help maximize profits?

We **negotiate loads**, **reduce empty miles**, and plan routes strategically so you spend less time waiting and more time driving.

Q4. Do you handle compliance and paperwork?

Yes. Our team assists with **broker packets**, **rate confirmations**, and other required documents.

Insurance Solutions FAQs

Q1. Do you sell insurance policies directly?

No, we act as a **service provider and consultant**. We connect you with the best **licensed insurance carriers** and help you understand your options.

Q2. Can you guarantee approval for Medicare or other insurance?

No, final approval depends on the **insurance carrier**. However, we guide you through the process to increase your chances of success.

Q3. How do I know which insurance plan is right for me?

We offer a **free consultation** where we compare options based on your health, family, and budget.

Q4. Do you provide both individual and family coverage?

Yes, we help with both personal and family insurance needs, including **Medicare, home, and auto plans**.

Web Development FAQs

Q1. How long does it take to build a website?

Q2. Do you provide ongoing support after the website goes live?

Yes, we offer **maintenance and updates** to keep your website secure and up-to-date.

Q3. Will my website be mobile-friendly?
